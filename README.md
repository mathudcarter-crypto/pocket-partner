# Pocket Partner - Home Outlet

Mobile web application for Home Outlet store employees to build customer quotes and learn product knowledge on the go.

## Features

### Quote Builder
- Scan price signs or enter SKUs manually
- Build shopping carts while walking with customers
- Send quotes via text or to BisTrack POS system
- All 1,622 products from catalog

### Product Learning
- Interactive product training with videos, quizzes, and selling tips
- Featured training products:
  - Paint (SKU: 123456789012)
  - LED Light (SKU: 234567890123)
  - Cordless Drill (SKU: 345678901234)
  - Smart Thermostat (SKU: 456789012345)
  - Deck Board (SKU: 567890123456)
  - Interior Door with Stacking Guide (SKU: 45284135)

## Usage

Simply open the app in Safari on your phone and grant camera permissions when prompted.

### For Quote Building:
1. Tap "Quote Builder" tab
2. Scan a price sign or enter SKU manually
3. Build your cart
4. Send quote via text or BisTrack

### For Product Learning:
1. Tap "Product Learning" tab
2. Scan a product or enter SKU manually
3. Learn features, selling tips, and best practices
4. Take knowledge check quiz

## Technical Details

- Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript - no dependencies needed
- Works offline after initial load
- Barcode scanning using QuaggaJS library
- Home Outlet brand colors (Yellow #FFD100 / Blue #0046E5)
